About Nelumbo

Meet Ms Maria Mberema

Ms Maria Mberema established Nelumbo Property Consultants CC in May 2021 with a clear purpose: to bring precision, integrity, and genuine expertise to Namibia's property services sector. As a 100% female owned business registered with the Business and Intellectual Property Authority of Namibia, the firm is a testament to what focused leadership and deep industry knowledge can achieve.

The Nelumbo team combines more than a decade of hands on experience in property valuations, management, real estate transactions, and project coordination. Drawing on both local market knowledge and international technical support, the firm is positioned to serve a wide range of clients from private individuals through to government ministries and multinational enterprises.

Every engagement is approached with the same commitment: to understand the client's situation thoroughly and to deliver a service that is accurate, accountable, and aligned with their specific property goals.

Advanced Project
Leadership and Strategies

The Nelumbo Advanced Project Leadership and Strategies programme equips property professionals with the tools, frameworks, and field experience needed to lead complex development projects with confidence. Participants work alongside experienced practitioners, gaining exposure to real project environments, stakeholder coordination, and the decision-making processes that determine outcomes on the ground.
