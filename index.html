About Nelumbo

Meet Ms Maria Mberema

Ms Maria Mberema established Nelumbo Property Consultants CC in May 2021 with a clear purpose: to bring precision, integrity, and genuine expertise to Namibia's property services sector. As a 100% female owned business registered with the Business and Intellectual Property Authority of Namibia, the firm is a testament to what focused leadership and deep industry knowledge can achieve.

The Nelumbo team combines more than a decade of hands on experience in property valuations, management, real estate transactions, and project coordination. Drawing on both local market knowledge and international technical support, the firm is positioned to serve a wide range of clients from private individuals through to government ministries and multinational enterprises.

Every engagement is approached with the same commitment: to understand the client's situation thoroughly and to deliver a service that is accurate, accountable, and aligned with their specific property goals.

Advanced Project
Leadership and Strategies

Nelumbo Property Consultants CC successfully delivered the Project Management Training programme titled Advanced Project Leadership and Strategies, hosted by NIPAM Namibia on 23 and 24 October 2025. Ms Maria Mberema served as lead trainer throughout the programme, guiding internal management staff through advanced project management methodologies, strategic planning techniques, and leadership frameworks tailored to complex organisational environments.

The two day programme equipped participants with advanced project management skills spanning the full project lifecycle: from foundational principles and detailed planning through to human resources management, quality assurance, procurement strategy, and risk management. Interactive sessions, case based discussions, and practical tools enabled each participant to develop genuine confidence in managing complex and high stakes projects.

This programme reflects the depth of expertise that Ms Mberema brings to every professional development engagement, and Nelumbo's commitment to equipping property and project management professionals with the skills they need to lead with clarity and purpose.
